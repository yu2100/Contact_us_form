About Chicken Recipe:

An American cutlet like dish with large and tender chicken pieces marinated well, bursting with flavours of spices.It is sometimes asssociated with the Southern cuisine of the United States. It is breaded and fried with a technique similar to the more common fried chicken,hence "chicken-fried".When deap-fried, it is ususally referred to as "country fried steak".

Chicken pieces are sizzled on a pan till they're cooked just right. Just you can cook for a lazy weekend dinner!

Preparation Time

Preparation time: 20mins

Cook time: 15mins

Feeds: 2 people

Ingredients of chicken Steak

Ingredients Measurements
Chicken breasts 4 large chicken breasts (boneless)
eggs 2 Eggs (slightly whisked)
Ginger 1 tsp ginger paste
Garlic 1 tsp garlic paste
Onions 1 cup onions, finely chopped
Coriander 2 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
Green Chillies 2 green chillies, finely chopped
Black pepper 1 tsp black pepper powder
Flour flour (enough to make a thin coating)
Vinegar 1 tbsp vinegar
Salt to taste salt
Oil Oil (to pan fry)

Directions

How to make Chicken Steak

  1. Wipe the chicken breasts and beat with a mallet or knife to flatten. Cut each into two pieces.
  2. Mix the chicken with the other ingredients, except oil.
  3. Put aside to marinate for 2 hours or so.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or grill with a thin layer of oil.
  5. When hot, place as many pieces of chicken without them touching each other, turning over at once.
  6. If your dish is not big enough to take in all the pieces together, you may have to do them in two lots.
  7. Lower the heat and cook till tender and brown on both sides, turning when one side is done. Serve hot.
